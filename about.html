About Omkar Executive Hotel

Omkar Executive Hotel is a premium hospitality destination located in the heart of Aurangabad. Known for its comfortable stay, modern facilities, and exceptional service, our hotel is the perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers.

Our hotel features well-appointed luxury rooms, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, 24/7 front desk support, and daily housekeeping to ensure a pleasant stay.

Guests can enjoy delicious meals at our multi-cuisine restaurant and organize events in our spacious conference and banquet halls. Whether you're here for a short visit or a long stay, we guarantee comfort, cleanliness, and a warm welcome.
